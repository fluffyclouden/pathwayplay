You Made It.


I had a lot of trouble making this website. Two laptops of mine broke down, and I had to make this site on a laptop thats about 10 years old. I thought about giving up.


But I Learned Something.


Our bodies perform these pathways every day to keep us alive. To keep us regulated, functioning and well. I learned that it is a miracle that we're here. Every cell in our bodies are fighting for us. These pathways are probably happening right now.


So who am I to not fight, too?


Remember, as you go through your own journey, life is like a metabolic pathway. Maybe there's an inhibitor making the process seem a bit more difficult, but we always find a way to bypass our obstacles on the cellular level. So let your own cells be your inspiration. Continue your journey, step by step, path by path.


Yours truly, Kris (creator of PathwayPlay)
