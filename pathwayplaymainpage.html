↻ !! Your Biochemical Pathways Tour Guide

Welcome to PathwayPlay, your roadmap to exploring the metabolic pathways in our bodies and other organisms. We will walk through each pathway like the yellow-brick road, paying close attention to the connections and shortcuts for each intermediate, and the activators and inhibitors (stop and go signs) for each road. Our body goes through these paths every single day, unbeknowst to us. Let's take some time to understand ourselves better on a biochemical level!

How to Navigate 〇

Use the MetaboloMap to explore key metabolic pathways. MetaboloMap walks you through a total of 8 possible pathways. Click on any pathway button to load each walkthrough back to the main page.

Use the top 'membrane bar' to check out some resources such as extra readings, the FAQs and key concepts. You can also submit questions.
